About Us

Our long term goal is to create an application that will prepare college students for getting a job when they graduate. Our product could be a piece of software that will suggest resume edits for maximizing the chances of recruitment. Unlike other format checking software, the program will be tailored towards technical / professional language used in resumes. For our product vision we chose to create an application that provides information about applying for jobs. A long term goal I would like to achieve is an implementation that helps the user build a resume based on the jobs that they are interested in. As well as this I would like to implement something that provides information about jobs they are interested in. Such as minimum education requirements, common questions about interviews in this field, and what will be expected of the user in this job.
Features:
  1. Job category selection
  2. Suggestion of information to add to resume depending job category
  3. Technical & Professional suggestions
  4. Resume Examples
  5. Information about relevant jobs
